About the Competition

The Sh Ali Sufi International Quran Qira'at Competition is one of the most prestigious events dedicated to the art of Quranic recitation. Established in 2005, this annual competition brings together the world's most talented Qaris to showcase the diversity and beauty of the Ten Qira'at (recitation styles).

Our mission is to preserve and promote the authentic methods of Quran recitation as passed down through generations of reciters, while encouraging excellence in Tajweed (proper pronunciation) and the melodic delivery of the Divine Word.

Each year, the competition travels to a different host country, promoting cultural exchange and unity through the Holy Quran. This year's competition will be held in Istanbul, Turkey, and will feature participants from over 40 countries.

Competition Categories

  • Hafs 'an 'Asim (Main Category)
  • Warsh 'an Nafi'
  • Qalun 'an Nafi'
  • Al-Duri 'an Abu 'Amr
  • Youth Category (Under 18)
  • Women's Category
  • Memorization Competition (30 Juz)

Previous Years Competitions

Explore the history and legacy of our competition through the years.

2023 Competition - Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

The 18th edition was hosted in Kuala Lumpur with participation from 48 countries. Special focus was given to the Warsh recitation style this year.

Highlights:

  • Record participation: 248 competitors
  • New category introduced: Complete Memorization (30 Juz)
  • Special guest: Sheikh Abdul Rahman Al-Sudais
  • Winner: Muhammad Ali Hassan (Egypt)

2022 Competition - Dubai, UAE

Held in the iconic Dubai World Trade Centre, this edition featured the largest women's category participation to date.

Highlights:

  • First hybrid competition (in-person and online)
  • Focus on Qalun recitation style
  • Winner: Ahmed Al-Mansouri (Saudi Arabia)

2021 Competition - Cairo, Egypt (Virtual)

Due to pandemic restrictions, the competition was held virtually from Cairo with participants joining from their home countries.

Highlights:

  • First fully virtual competition
  • Live streaming reached 2.5 million viewers
  • Winner: Fatima Al-Jaber (UAE)

2020 Competition - Istanbul, Turkey

The competition was held in the historic city of Istanbul with special emphasis on youth participation.

Highlights:

  • Focus on youth development programs
  • Cultural exchange program between participants
  • Winner: Yusuf Ahmed (Egypt)

2019 Competition - Mecca, Saudi Arabia

A landmark competition held in the holy city of Mecca during Ramadan, attracting unprecedented global attention.

Highlights:

  • Held during the last ten nights of Ramadan
  • Special Taraweeh prayers led by competition winners
  • Winner: Abdullah Al-Mansouri (Saudi Arabia)

2018 Competition - Jakarta, Indonesia

The first competition held in Southeast Asia, celebrating the rich tradition of Quran recitation in the region.

Highlights:

  • Largest audience: over 10,000 attendees
  • Focus on Indonesian recitation styles
  • Winner: Omar Farouk (Indonesia)
